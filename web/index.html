Snow White Spiti

Premium Spiti Snow Ride

Join us on a thrilling snow tour on a motorcycle from Chandigarh to Spiti Valley is an exhilarating adventure, blending the charm of the open road with the breathtaking landscapes of the Himalayas. The journey begins in Chandigarh, where riders set out on their powerful bikes, navigating through the bustling city before ascending into the snowcapped peaks. As riders traverse the winding roads, they witness a mesmerizing transformation in scenery, from the lush greenery of the lower Himalayas to the stark, snow-covered beauty of Spiti.

HIGHLIGHTS OF THIS TOUR

  • 9 day motorcycle adventure
  • Destination focus on Spiti, Sangla, Tirthan valleys
  • Off road opportunities and stunning stretches of tarmac
  • Jalori Pass -The highest motorable road in this circuit
  • Fully aided tour with backup team
  • Travel through Winter Spiti
  • Comfortable stays, a variety of food and local subject matter resources
